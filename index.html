VideoNotes

VideoNotes is the app that turns any YouTube video into a clear, searchable, and summarized document. Just paste the URL and the app does the rest.

Video to Text

Simple and direct. Copy the link of any YouTube video and let the app automatically convert it into text. Save, share, or summarize the content however you like—no hassle.

AI to PDF

Save time with Artificial Intelligence. With VideoNotes, AI automatically summarizes those long videos you usually postpone due to lack of time, offering you a condensed version in just a few lines so you don’t miss what matters most.
